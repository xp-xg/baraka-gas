2. LPG Gas Composition

Component Percentage (%) Properties
Propane (C₃H₈) 30-70% Lower vapor pressure, better for cold weather
Butane (C₄H₁₀) 30-70% Higher energy content, better for warm weather
Odourant 0.004% Ethyl mercaptan for leak detection
Note: The exact propane/butane ratio varies by season. Winter blend has higher propane for better vaporization in cold temperatures. Summer blend has higher butane for economy.
